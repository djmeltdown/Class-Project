About Me

One thing that people notice about me is that I have many hobbies. In the past, I've been a full time DJ, taught myself how to cook authentic Japanese ramen, done photography, and dabbled in pottery.
These days I spend most of my spare time keeping up with new designer board games and activities surrounding that. I spend a lot of time on Board Game Geek.
I mention board games as my main hobby, because the two main activities that take up my spare time are done in support of them.
Currently, 3D Printing board game inserts and upgrades, and painting 28mm miniatures are my two main hobbies.
They kind of go hand in hand, since I paint what I print.

For 3D Printing I use a Creality Ender 3 Pro.I've upgraded it with an auto bed-leveling sensor, glass print bed, and metal extruder.
Also, all the orange parts are things I've printed and added to the machine!

I get all of my files off of Thingiverse.
If you can think of something, chances are someone's already designed it so all you have to do is print it!

The main thing I've been printing over the last 6 months are dungeon tiles for Dungeons and Dragons.
Each square is 1 inch and represents 5 feet of floor in the dungeon. Miniatures will be placed on the squares and follow rules for movement

Here's an example of the miniatures that I paint.Their bases are 1 inch in diameter and fit perfectly!
