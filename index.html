Chris Guild's Web Page About Miniature Painting

Welcome to my page! I hope you enjoy your visit as you navigate around the site. I hoping this will be a useful resource for those that are interesting in getting
started. Below is a picture of my work station, and a few of my favorite paint jobs so far! Check out the tutorial page to see how I do it, and visit the gallery to see
more of my work! The videos page has some very useful videos that helped me get started and helped me think about how miniature painting could be done. After you've watched
videos, you'll want to head over to the glossary so you can translate what all those technical terms that you just heard into real meaning. Finally, if you'd like some
recommendations on supplies, check out the Links page. I've complied some hyperlinks to useful items. Thanks for visiting!
